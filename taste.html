    • OVERVIEW

    A website created when local businesses were facing hardships after quarantine. This website shows people who are visiting LA an alternative side they can choose. Rather than going to the most famous tourist stops, people can also explore the numerous small businesses and family owned restaurants that make up the culture of the city.

    Github Website

    1. IDEATE

      Problem:

    • lack of representation of small businesses during travel
    • struggling local businesses especially after the pandemic
    • hard to find photos of local travel locations

      Solution:

    • build a platform to represent small businesses
    • give exposure to these businesses
    • have a gallery to share photos taken by other travelers

    2. COMPETITOR RESEARCH

      Yelp:

    • showed the most popular place only, didn't showcase local/family restaurants
    • liked how informative and interactive it was with location,photos, ratings
    • a lot of content, can be overwhelming

      VSCO:

    • great place to store photos, especially travel photos
    • UI is hard to use and get used to at first
    • would like to have a gallery just for travel

    3. MOODBOARD

    • wanted the vibes to be geared towards film
    • black, white, and blue color palette

    4. WIREFRAME

    • first few ideations
    • made a lot of changes moving forward

    5. CODING!

    • used html, css, and javascript to make the website come to life.
