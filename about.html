    Hi! I'm Rachel Chen

    I'm a second year Computer Science and Design student with a concentration in Interaction Design and a minor in Brand Management at Northeastern University in Boston, Massachusetts. I'm originally from Los Angeles, California, which is how I became so accustomed to so many different creative outlets.

    I've always had a love for art and found myself being the creative one in group projects. I didn't know how I could incorporate art into my career until I discovered UI/UX design Through my work, I hope to create advances in the digital world and positively impact users.. I'm passionate about creating seamless and user-centric experiences!

    My academic journey centers around full stack development, software engineering, web development, and UI/UX design.

    I'm a very interdisciplinary individual. Growing up, I was deeply involved in health sciences and even though it's not my professional path anymore, I still have a passion for the healthcare industry and I am enthusiastic about the potential that arises when technology, healthcare, and design come together.
