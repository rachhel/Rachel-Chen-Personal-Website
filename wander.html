    • OVERVIEW

    A project created during my Exerience and Interaction class. Tasked with redesigning a travel agency, I wanted to focus on how trip planning within groups can be more efficient. Wanderluxe strive to make traveling easier for groups of younger individuals. Planning with large group is difficult, WL wants to redesign traveling to make it easier!

    High Fidelity Presentation

    1. IDEATE

      Problem:

    • college students usually like to travel on a budget
    • hard to collabrate with friends about finances and trip details
    • photos get lost in collections and is shoved deep in your phone

      Solution:

    • filter out and cap a budget to find a spot that works well for everyone
    • be able to add friends into the trip and share finances throughout the trip
    • users are able to create an albume for each location and share it. No followers, just for documenting the trip.

    2. COMPETITOR RESEARCH

      Lonely Planet:

    • pages were too lengthy and wordy
    • overwhelming due to the influx of information
    • really liked the use of images and videos

      Let's go:

    • good use of the nav bar and good info
    • don't like the aesthetics of the site
    • main page takes too much of the space, not user friendly

    3. MOODBOARD

    • wanted the vibes to be like a scrapbook since half the mission is to document the trip
    • through warm and pastel tones, it gives an exciting and lively feel, perfect for traveling.

    4. WIREFRAME

    • first few ideations
    • many different sketches and lots of brainstorming.

    5. High-Fidelity!

    • Using Figma, I created a high fidelty wireframe of the site. I incorporated photos and different elements to make the website design that I envisioned.
