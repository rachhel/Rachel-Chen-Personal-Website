    • OVERVIEW

    A final project as a Kode With Klossy Scholar, I wanted to create something that meant a lot to me. And, that was mental health. Many students have extremely busy schedules and push themselves to overwork. Through Moodivity, people can be productive while focusing on their well-being. It helps these students plan out their weeks, jot down to-do lists, and time their study sessions. It also incorporates self-care by adding breaks, motivation, and serotonin pictures to boost users' moods

      Problem:

    • Students are overwhelmed and don't understand when it's time to take a break.
    • It's hard for some students to mention that they need help and they aren't really taught in school how to take care of their mental health.
    • Some students need extra help when it comes to planning their days and staying on task.

      Solution:

    • build a platform incorporate both mental health and planning
    • make it user friendly and welcoming
    • remove the stigma behind asking for help and mental health
